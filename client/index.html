What is Adrenaline Escapes?

You and your team are given a mission and enter into a themed room. You have 60 minutes to discover clues, solve puzzles, answer riddles, and manipulate contraptions in order to complete your mission. The fastest team to escape gets bragging rights, and those that don't make it still have a blast working together and sharing some laughs.

Bank Heist

You are Southeastern Manitoba’s most successful crime syndicate. Today, you’ll be facing your greatest challenge yet; a heist at the Stoney Brook Bank.

Your recon team has just completed phase one of the heist — they have paid the janitor a generous sum of money in return for granting you access to the bank manager’s office. They also report that they have suspended the security system for 60 minutes.

They have hacked into the bank manager’s computer, and will use it to display how much time you have remaining. They will also be digging through files and emails and will provide to you any helpful information they come across, should you require it.

Once you have gained access to the vault, grab as much money as you can, and escape through the emergency exit before the alarm sounds. Good luck. You’ll need it.

What is Adrenaline Escapes?

You and your team are given a mission and enter into a themed room. You have 60 minutes to discover clues, solve puzzles, answer riddles, and manipulate contraptions in order to complete your mission. The fastest team to escape gets bragging rights, and those that don't make it still have a blast working together and sharing some laughs.

Pirate's Plunder

The Red Dagger, the most notorious Pirate Ship on the high seas, has docked at Tortuga to resupply after a long journey.

Listening in on their usual tales of pillage and plunder, you overhear one particularly impaired pirate let slip that there is treasure on the ship at this very moment!

Do you dare sneak aboard to steal the loot before the crew returns? If you do, you had best be a savvy sea dog, or you’ll find yourself walking the plank.

Bank Heist

NOTE: The last day to book this room is September 7th, 2018!

Your team is Southeastern Manitoba’s most successful crime syndicate and you're going to be facing your greatest challenge yet; a heist at the Stoney Brook Bank.

You have gained access to the bank manager’s office and the security system has been suspended for 60 minutes.

Gain access to the vault, grab as much cash as you can and escape before the alarm sounds. If you don't make it, your future will involve the bare walls and barred windows of a jail cell. So get out in time, and cement your place in the history books.
